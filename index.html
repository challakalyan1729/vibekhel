Our Story

Passion for Street Sports

Vibekhel was born from a simple idea: to bring back the joy and camaraderie of traditional Indian street sports.

Our Mission & Values

At Vibekhel, we believe in the power of sports to unite communities, foster healthy competition, and create lasting memories. We are dedicated to promoting street sports culture across India, providing a platform for enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels.

  • **Community Building:** Connecting people through shared passion for sports.
  • **Inclusivity:** Events for all ages, genders, and skill levels.
  • **Fair Play:** Emphasizing sportsmanship and respect.
  • **Healthy Lifestyle:** Encouraging physical activity and well-being.
