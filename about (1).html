About Vibekhel

Vibekhel is dedicated to bringing together street sports enthusiasts across India. Our mission is to create a vibrant community where players of gully cricket, kabaddi, chess, volleyball, and more can connect, compete, and celebrate the spirit of local sports.

Founded with a passion for grassroots sports, Vibekhel provides a platform to discover events, organize matches, and build lasting friendships. Join us in revolutionizing how street sports are experienced in India!
